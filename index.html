• I arrived at the pound as a stray. They called my original owner who was very shocked that I had ended up with us. Just 3 weeks earlier he had given me to a new family, with a promise of a good and permanent home... Unfortunately, it didn't go as planned. I was reaaaally stressed. I jumped and flipped around, they said I am becoming a risk for myself. I didn't have to wait long thought, because I was a really good boy and a lot of people like me, so at last I moved in with a nice loving family.

    Yours truly, Zeus

  • After quite a time spend in the pound, I finally moved out to a cute family who has experience with several different dogs. They live in the country and have lots of fenced grounds, meaning I have all the space to run around and have fun. I had to go to a training before I get the chance to go there, of course, but at the end of the summer I moved in with my new loving family. I cannot be happier!

    Yours truly, Kenzo

  • My name is Gilbert and I want to tell you my short love story. I was in the pound when a caring lady came along. She regularly comes by the pound but from the moment she met me it was like a love at first sight - she immediately became attached to me and I to her, and we created a special bond together. Thanks to that bonding and the fact that she couldn't get me out of her head, she decided to just adopt me and now I am only hers to love and care for. Dreams come true, don't they?

    Yours truly, Gilbert

  • Hello, my name is Dana and would like to share with my joy of finally founding my forever home. It has all the space for me to hop and jump and be myself, it also has another bunny for me to play with! We became fast friends and I am no longer locked in a small cage, for which I am grateful.

    Yours truly, Dana

  • My name is Mozart, like the famous human, and I was looking for a home with my friend Cuba, but after many considerations, the humans decided that the best thing for us would be to move out separately. I have therefore moved to Zealand, where I am going to stay with a couple who are used to Huskies like me. I will never be left alone at home and I hope my friend also finds his humans soon.

    Yours truly, Mozart like the famous human
